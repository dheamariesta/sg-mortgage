FAQ

  • Q: What are your loan amount and terms?

    A: Our term loans range from $5,000 – $500,000 over 3 – 36 month terms. Our lines of credit go up to $100,000. Payments are made over six months. The lines are revolving so as you pay down your balance, your available funds increase.

    A: Give us a call at (65)1234 5678 or email us your financial needs at support@sgmortgage.com and we will give you free consultation
  • Q: Can foreigners also take up loan from you?

    A: Definitely yes. We welcome all nationalities who wants to invest in Singapore properties (provided you have the necessary permit to stay in Singapore).
