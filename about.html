Our History

2003

Realising that getting a property mortgage was difficult, our founder stepped up and start SGMortgage, as the first online mortgage comparison portal in Singapore.

2010

SGMortgage received an investment sum of $1 Billion; awarded as start-up of the year.

2015

SGMortgage was registered for IPO in New York Stock Exchange

Now

We have catered more that 500,000 families and businesses in Singapore and have built solid relationships with major banks.
